Why digital banking?

Digital banking is no longer just about transactions.

It is about trust in moments that matter, intuitive systems across borders and seamless experiences in an increasingly global world.

Living and working in Australia showed me how financial systems directly shape everyday life.

That is exactly where I want to contribute.

🏦

Why Commerzbank?

What excites me about Commerzbank is the transformation of a traditional financial institution into a more digital, customer-focused organisation.

The combination of strong roots in the German Mittelstand, a broad private customer base and ongoing digital innovation creates a unique environment where change is not theoretical — but real.

I want to be part of that transformation.

🚀

Why the IDDP?

The International Digital Development Program stands out because it combines learning with real responsibility.

Rotations across different areas, international exposure and early ownership create exactly the environment I am looking for:

Not just understanding transformation — but actively shaping it.
